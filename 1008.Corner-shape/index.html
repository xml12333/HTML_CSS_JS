corner-shape playground

Try different shapes. Browsers without corner-shape support will show classic rounded corners via border-radius.

round
Default geometry—behaves like standard border-radius.
scoop
Concave corners; feels “carved in”.
bevel
Straight chamfer instead of a curve.
notch
Inset cut at the corner—retro UI vibes.
squircle
Superellipse-based corners (smooth, modern).
square
Cancels the rounding even with border-radius set.
mixed (TL TR BR BL)
Per-corner shapes: scoop, bevel, round, notch.
superellipse(4)
Numeric control—classic squircle feel.
superellipse(8)
Higher exponent → corners trend toward square.
